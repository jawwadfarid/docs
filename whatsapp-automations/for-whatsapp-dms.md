| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Who you message | Individual **users** (1:1)                                                  | WhatsApp groups                       |

| Service window  | The 24-hour rule applies for  & No service window for one-way notifications | No window limits                      |
