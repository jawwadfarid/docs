# Security

CodeWords takes a security-by-design approach to protecting your data. Our team is continuing to make investments so you can use CodeWords with confidence.

We're currently in the process of getting our SOC 2 Type II compliance.&#x20;

***

These are the security measures and processes we've implemented:&#x20;

**Business continuity**&#x20;

* Ongoing internal education and compliance updates&#x20;
* Comprehensive recovery plans
* Active communication procedures to our customers&#x20;
* Regular release notes and product updates



**Data governance and privacy**&#x20;

* Privacy by design principles&#x20;
* Transparent data handling&#x20;
* Regular access reviews and monitoring
* Data classification and retention policies

You can access our full [Privacy Policy](https://codewords.ai/privacy-policy) to read more.



**Security operations**

* Regular testing and code reviews
* Continuous integration of security measures in development pipelines
* Documented response procedures
* Automated alert systems for rapid response
